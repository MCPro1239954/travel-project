Nigeria (11 Day)

On this 11-day whirlwind adventure through Nigeria, you'll explore a nation pulsing with diversity, development, and dynamic human patterns. This trip hits major course units from urban development (Unit 6), to agriculture (Unit 5), cultural geography (Unit 3), economic systems (Unit 7), and political organization (Unit 4). It’s a human geographer’s dream itinerary.

  • Days 1-3: Arrival & Lagos
  • Days 4-5: Kaduna
  • Days 6-7: Kano
  • Days 8-9: Port Harcourt
  • Days 10-11: Abuja & Departure
